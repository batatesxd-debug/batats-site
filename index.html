Two Sides of Cybersecurity

  • Red Team → Attack & find weaknesses.
  • Blue Team → Defend & protect systems.

Red Team → Penetration Testing

  • Simulate real attacks on systems or networks.
  • Discover security flaws and report them ethically.

Blue Team → Network Security

  • The First Line of Defense:
  • 1 - Stop attacks
  • 2 - Detect fast
  • 3 - Reduce damage

Purple Team

  • The Bridge Between Red and Blue Team.
  • Turn every attack into a lesson for stronger defense.
